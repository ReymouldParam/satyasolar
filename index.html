FAQ

How Much Can I Save By Switching To Solar? +
Savings vary by location, usage, and system size. Residential users can save up to 90% on energy bills, while commercial and industrial savings depend on specific energy needs
Is There Any Government Incentive For Solar Panel Installation? −
Yes! Schemes like PM-KUSUM and the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana support solar adoption. While PM-KUSUM is more agriculture-focused, the Muft Bijli Yojana offers a subsidy of up to ₹78,000 for rooftop solar. Check with local authorities to explore your eligibility and benefits.
Will Solar Panels Work During Cloudy Days Or At Night? +
Solar panels can still produce energy on cloudy days, though at reduced efficiency. At night, power is drawn from battery storage or the grid to keep your home running smoothly.
Do Solar Panels Require A Lot Of Maintenance? +
No, solar panels need minimal upkeep, mainly occasional cleaning and routine checks to keep them operating at their best.
What Is The Payback Period For A Solar Investment? +
Most solar investments pay off within 5 to 10 years, depending on your system cost and energy savings, after which you enjoy years of virtually free, clean energy.
