FAQ

Things You Need To Know About Solution

How Much Can I Save By Switching To Solar? +
Savings vary based on your location, energy usage, and system size. Most users save significantly over time on their energy bills.
Is There Any Government Incentive For Solar Panel Installation? −
Many governments offer tax credits, rebates, and incentives for installing solar panels. Check with your local authorities for available programs.
Will Solar Panels Work During Cloudy Days Or At Night? +
Solar panels still generate power during cloudy days but less efficiently. At night, energy must come from storage or the grid.
Do Solar Panels Require A Lot Of Maintenance? +
No, they require minimal maintenance—just occasional cleaning and inspection to ensure peak performance.
What Is The Payback Period For A Solar Investment? +
Typically, the payback period ranges from 5 to 10 years depending on installation costs and energy savings.
